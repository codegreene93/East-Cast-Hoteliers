Train the Trainer


Consistency is key in creating guests for life. Training is key for displaying the standards expected to your team. Being able to deliver your message across to your team is integral to being a top performing establishment... Train the Trainer is a full day course that gives you the tools to be able to get the message across to your team. We focus on this in two ways. Identifying training needs and delivery design.


WSET


Profit margins on wine alone can be between 60-70% per glass. Profit margin on food typically falls somewhere between 0-15% in a restaurant. The Food and Beverage industry is notoriously competitive with 60% of new restaurants failing every year. A new restaurant to be successful an emphasis should really be put on the extras that a restaurant offers in order to keep a business profitable.
WSET gives a great foundation in your wine + spirit knowledge. This can really come in handy when it comes to food pairings and can really wow your guests.


Language of 5* service


How do you handle a complaint? How do you deal with a difficult customer? What makes the difference between having a good interaction with a guest and a truely memorable experience? These are the type of questions that we answer in "The language of 5* service".
This full day intensive course takes your team through some of the questions that you as a manager may find difficult to put into words. Tailored to each organisations business we will draw from out own personal experience and convey that in a way that aligns with your companys brand standards. We want to create a dialogue your team to wow your guests along as some non verbal ques that you would like them to pick up on.
