Who We Are:


A team of 5 hospitality professionals all with international experience from Dubai to Washington DC, ECH has now come together to open it's first office in Dublin, Ireland. We are talented and motivated professionals with a proven track record wishing to pass on our knowledge of wealth to you to ensure your business remains profitable. Having engaged in many prestigious and transformational projects, our clients can draw on the strength of our experience and commitment to their success which begins at the top and threads through the entire organization.
